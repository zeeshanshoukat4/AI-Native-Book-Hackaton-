# Chapter 5 - Topic 1: The Future of AI-Native

The AI-Native paradigm is still in its early days, but it is already clear that it will have a profound impact on the future of technology and society. In this topic, we will explore some of the long-term trends that are shaping the future of AI-Native, and we will consider what it will mean to live in a world where AI is ubiquitous.

We will discuss the potential for AI to solve some of the world's most pressing problems, from climate change to disease. We will also consider the ethical challenges that will need to be addressed as AI becomes more powerful and autonomous.

By the end of this topic, you will have a better understanding of the long-term vision for AI-Native, and you will be better prepared to navigate the opportunities and challenges of this exciting new era.
